# Welcome to Kayf

We built Kayf because we were tired of watching progress bars.

Every time we sent a prompt to ChatGPT or Claude, we'd sit there, watching the cursor blink, reading words appear one by one. It wasn't productive. It wasn't restful. It was just... waiting.

Kayf solves that. Send your prompt, go do something enjoyable for 30 seconds, and come back to a finished answer. It sounds almost too simple. That's kind of the point.

## What's in v1

- Automatic switching to your break tab when a prompt is sent
- Auto-return when your AI finishes responding
- Support for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and DeepSeek
- YouTube and Netflix auto pause/resume
- Zero data collection

## What's coming

We're working on custom break activities, a focus mode, and browser-wide session stats. No timeline — we ship when it's good.

Thanks for being here.
